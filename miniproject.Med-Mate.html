About

Our Goal: Med-Mate is designed to reduce the risk of missed medications and help people live healthier lives through smart technology. Our mission is to automate medication management in a safe, secure, and simple way.

Uses of Med-Mate:

  • Schedule and remind daily doses
  • Track skipped or taken medications
  • Monitor inventory and set refill alerts
  • Connect caregivers or family for remote monitoring
  • Export health reports for doctors and clinics
